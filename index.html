About MaxRen

MaxRen is a web application designed to enhance daily interactions within communities, starting with college dormitories. By fostering a culture of acknowledgment and motivation, MaxRen encourages equitable contribution to communal living, reducing conflicts and enhancing the residential experience.

Core Features

  • User Group Creation
  • Long Term Goal Setting
  • Routine Effort Tracking
  • Community Acknowledgment
