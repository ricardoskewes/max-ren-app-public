MaxRen

A Revolutionary App for Harmonious Community Living

Empowering Roommates, Transforming Residential Life

Developed by Nicholas and Ricardo, juniors at Harvard College, MaxRen is designed to improve roommate interactions and foster a cohesive living environment. By encouraging equitable contributions to shared responsibilities, MaxRen aims to reduce conflicts and enhance the dormitory experience.

Features

  • User Groups Creation
  • Long-term Goals Setting
  • Community Acknowledgment
  • Routine Effort Tracking
